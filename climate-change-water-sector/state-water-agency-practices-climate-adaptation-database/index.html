State Water Agency Practices for Climate Adaptation Database

The compilation of practices featured below highlight some of the innovative actions or planning efforts by various state water management agencies that help address a changing climate. Showcasing a diverse array of climate adaptation related practices in several programmatic areas and geographic locations around the country can help encourage the adoption of useful models across state agencies.

January 3, 2017 - We are pleased to announce that a second round of practices has been added to the compilation below.

If you would like to submit a climate related practice from your state agency for consideration, please contact water_climate_change@epa.gov for more details.

To download the documents, scroll to the bottom of the page.
