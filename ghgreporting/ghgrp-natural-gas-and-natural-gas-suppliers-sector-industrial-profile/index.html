Greenhouse Gas Reporting Program (GHGRP)

GHGRP Natural Gas and Natural Gas Liquids Suppliers Sector Industrial Profile

EPA's Greenhouse Gas Reporting Program periodically produces detailed profiles of the various industries that report under the program. The profiles available for download below contain detailed analyses for the Natural Gas and Natural Gas Suppliers industry. These analyses include estimates of GHGRP coverage, emissions trends (including discussion), emissions by state, gas, and process, the number of reporters whose emissions fall above and below certain threshold values, and monitoring methods used. Each profile contains data from the indicated year back to 2011, and in some cases, 2010.

You will need Adobe Reader to view some of the files on this page. See EPA’s About PDF page to learn more.
